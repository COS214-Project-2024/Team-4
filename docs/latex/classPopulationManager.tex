\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Manages the population of citizens. 

The \doxylink{classPopulationManager}{Population\+Manager} class manages the population of citizens, including adding, removing, and updating citizens. It also handles job assignments, relationship management, and satisfaction updates.
