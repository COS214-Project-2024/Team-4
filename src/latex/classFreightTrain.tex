\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A class representing a freight train. 

The \doxylink{classFreightTrain}{Freight\+Train} class inherits from the \doxylink{classTrain}{Train} class and includes additional attributes specific to freight trains, such as weight and length. It also manages collections of \doxylink{classInsideRoad}{Inside\+Road} and \doxylink{classFreightTrain}{Freight\+Train} objects.
