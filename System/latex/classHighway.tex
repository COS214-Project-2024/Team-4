\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a highway with a speed limit. 

The \doxylink{classHighway}{Highway} class inherits from the \doxylink{classRoad}{Road} class and includes additional attributes and methods specific to highways, such as a speed limit and lists of inside roads and other highways.
